THE CONVOCATION DAY.

The convocation is the ceremony before graduations in every year which involves of academicians students, alumni, benefactors ,and several industry proffessionals as stakeholders of the University community.

Its the time to exchange views on topic issues that touch teaching , research, and proffessional development. This Convocation was established by Ms Nyambilila Minga up to now.

THE SHORT BRIEFY ABOUT CONVOCATION DAY

The convocation day involves of exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching, research, and proffessional development and also awarding prizes to the best students also awarding certificate of merits to EASTCSO leaders and also sharing experiance between them.

The example of previous convocation was the 6th convocation in which select the topic which was said that "The role official statistics, also big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania" as the standing point reflect on the theme of this year's 18 of November African Statistics Day. The African Statistics Day is celebrated each year on 18th of November. The commemoration aims among others, to increase public awareness about the important role which statistics play in all aspects of social and economic development. This year's convocation aims to understand the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) and the potential role statistics and big data plays in discovering markets and serving customers across Africa.
